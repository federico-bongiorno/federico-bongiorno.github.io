Federico Bongiorno

I am a PhD student at the London School of Geometry and Number Theory working in algebraic geometry under the supervision of Professor Paolo Cascini
. My doctoral dissertaion on Infinitesimal groupoids and quotient spaces may be found here.

Research

My research interests include algebraic foliations, infinitesimal groupoids and intersection theory on singular varieties.
